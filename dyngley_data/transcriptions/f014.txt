					<title select="#rs-ail">for the ache of <rs>disease in the raynes</rs> of the bakke</title>
				</head>
				<p>Take a <measure>quarter of yard</measure> of redde scarlett &amp; stycche therto the woll of a blakke shepe thatt growyth apon the brest and then take pouder of [?]aunders &amp; 
				strew over the sayd woll &amp; also pouder of lycoras and after that don to over spryngyll the same with oyle of oylyff and that plastur shall restore nature. 
				And ease the pacient of his disease so it be layed to his raynes &amp; <measure>every wyke</measure> the same plastre to be renewed with same pouder &amp; oyle. 
				And so to use it till he be hoole &amp;c.</p>
