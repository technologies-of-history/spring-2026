# My Podcast: Writing — The Core of Every Communication
Think about it. Every single time humans wanted to communicate better, 
<!--more-->
they built something new. The printing press. The telegraph. Radio. TV. 
The internet. Your phone. And every single one of them, was built on writing? 
That's exactly what I discuss in my podcast. So let's find out!
