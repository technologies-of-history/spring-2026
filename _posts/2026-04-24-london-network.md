I chose this database because I thought it would be interesting to focus on women’s relationship to knowledge in London between 1640 and 1670. This was an interesting time with lots of changes happening so I was curious to see how people communicated and stayed connected through letters. I was also curious about how women were represented in these communication networks. A research question this data might answer is: how often were women communicating compared to men at this time and did women’s connections change over time between 1640 and 1670?
